About Our Firm

DHANANI & JAIN, approved by The Institute of Chartered Accountants of India, is founded on the principle of standardizing professional work and expanding services to international boundaries. We view each engagement as uniquely important.

Our multidisciplinary team comprises Chartered Accountants, Company Secretaries, Advocates, and Cost & Work Management Accountants, ensuring comprehensive financial and legal support for our clients.

Our Mission

To retain clients with our service, depth of knowledge, and deep understanding of their business, personal, and financial goals.

Our Vision

To be an innovative, nationally and globally recognized firm, connecting clients to knowledge and resources.

Our Values

Integrity, Excellence, and Teamwork drive every engagement, viewing each uniquely important.

Our Leadership

CA. Karan Jain (FCA, B.com)

Fellow Member of the ICAI

CA Karan Jain is a Fellow Member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI), recognized for his deep expertise in Direct Taxation, GST, Corporate Laws, and Audit & Assurance. He leads the firm’s Taxation and Corporate Compliance practice and heads the Mumbai Branch, driving strategic growth and operational excellence.

He brings extensive experience in handling complex ROC and regulatory compliances, statutory and internal audits, and end-to-end tax advisory. His expertise spans scrutiny assessments, appellate proceedings, and representation before statutory authorities, where he is known for delivering practical, defensible, and result-oriented solutions.

He has served a diverse client base across textiles, construction, manufacturing, and financial services sectors, consistently delivering high-quality, value-driven outcomes aligned with business objectives.

CA. Kaushal Dhanani (FCA, B.com)

Fellow Member of the ICAI

CA Kaushal Dhanani is a Fellow Member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI), with core expertise in Audit & Assurance, Banking Audits, SME IPO Compliance, Securities Regulations, and Indirect Taxation. He is a NISM Certified Professional, a Xero Certified Professional (Level 2), and a qualified GST Practitioner.

He brings in-depth experience in SEBI regulatory compliances, internal control evaluation, financial and legal due diligence, and capital market assignments. His professional exposure includes handling statutory bank audits, stock audits, inspection audits, and SME IPO listing and concurrent audits, ensuring strong compliance and robust financial governance.

He also possesses significant expertise in GST, including compliance, advisory, assessments, and litigation support, delivering comprehensive and practical indirect tax solutions to clients.
